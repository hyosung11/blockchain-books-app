What is blockchain?

Simply put, blockchain is software. An online distributed ledger, blockchain is software for keeping track of any kind of data. Bitcoin is the mainstream manifestations of its potential. Blockchains store information immmutably. The immutable nature of the unhackable algorithms used to store transactions on its ledger makes blockchains "trustless." At its core blockchain technology makes it possible to shift from centralized to decentralized models in all apsects of our lives.
